What to Expect

A Typical Day on the Job

Here's exactly what showing up for a Pressure Point job looks like.

Meet Up & Load Out Meet at the job site or equipment staging area. We load the trailer, confirm what's getting cleaned, and head out together.
Set Up & Clean Connect the equipment, mix chemicals as needed, and work the job from start to finish. We move efficiently and don't cut corners.
Walk-Through Before leaving, we review the work together. If anything's off, we fix it. The standard is the same on every job.
Pack Up & Get Paid Wrap the day, load everything up, and get paid. Straightforward, no waiting weeks for your money.
Who We're Looking For

What You Need to Bring

The bar isn't complicated. We just need people we can count on.

  • Reliable and on time. This matters more than anything else.
  • Willing to work outdoors in South Carolina summer heat (and sometimes rain).
  • Good attitude and a strong work ethic. We move fast on jobs.
  • Able to handle equipment, move hoses, and stay on your feet for a few hours.
  • Valid driver's license. A plus, but not required to get started.
  • No experience needed. We train you on everything from day one.
