How we price (what affects your quote)

  • Per-square-foot rate: Most flatwork and siding uses $0.20-$0.40 / sq ft depending on condition (not a mystery “job size”).
  • Buildup & staining: Red clay, tire marks, heavy algae, and shaded mildew take longer and use more chemistry than a light rinse.
  • Surface & porosity: Smooth new concrete costs less than rough, aged, or paver surfaces. Soft wash siding is quoted after photos.
  • Access: Tight side yards, long hose runs, gates, and obstacles add time.
  • Travel: Columbia metro included; text us if you are outside our usual Blythewood / Dentsville radius.
  • Owner review: Parker approves every quote: student-owned, but not a call-center estimate.

A lower price often costs more. Harsh pressure on siding or fragile stucco can cause damage that costs far more than the wash you skipped. We adjust pressure and chemistry to the surface. That craftsmanship is built into our pricing.

Our process & equipment

SC Pressure Point is student-owned, but every job is run with owner oversight. We use professional-grade equipment and adjust pressure for the surface. driveways get more agitation; siding gets soft wash treatment when needed (low pressure at the wall, similar to a strong garden hose fan). We pre-wet plants when using cleaning solutions and rinse surrounding landscaping after flatwork. Older neighborhoods like Shandon and Rosewood often have softer mortar and aged stucco. Those jobs get slower chemistry and gentler rinsing, which is reflected in the quote.

Busy season & scheduling

Peak demand in the Midlands is usually late spring through early summer and again in fall when pollen and leaves hit roofs and concrete. Pricing is based on scope, not how full the calendar is. Text early to lock your preferred date. We offer weekday and many weekend slots.

Glossary

Soft wash
Low-pressure application of cleaning solution, then a controlled rinse. Safer for siding and delicate trim.
Per square foot
How we quote most flatwork and siding: you pay for measurable area, not a vague job label.
Surface cleaner
Tool for even, streak-free cleaning on driveways and sidewalks.
Dwell time
How long solution sits to loosen organics before rinsing (varies by temperature and shade).
PSI
Pressure at the pump; we turn down effective pressure at delicate surfaces.
