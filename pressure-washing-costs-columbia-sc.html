What affects your price

Two driveways the same size can quote differently. Here is what moves the number up or down:

  • Buildup and staining — Thick algae, mildew, tire marks, and red clay need more time and chemistry than a light maintenance wash.
  • Surface type and porosity — Older, rough concrete absorbs more solution than smooth newer flatwork. Brick and paver joints add detail work.
  • Access and obstacles — Tight side yards, parked cars, landscaping, and long hose runs slow the job and can add labor.
  • Height and delicacy — Siding, soffit, and gutters often need soft washing instead of high pressure, which changes method and time.
  • Total square footage — Larger areas sometimes qualify for a slightly lower per-sq-ft rate; very small jobs may have a minimum visit charge.
  • Location in the Midlands — We serve Columbia, Lexington, Irmo, and surrounding towns from one crew; travel is usually included in metro quotes.

When you text a photo, we are looking at these factors — not guessing from a generic chart. That is why a free estimate beats a one-size-fits-all online calculator.
