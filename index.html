Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our power washing services.

Our pricing typically ranges from $0.20 to $0.30 per square foot, depending on the surface type and condition. We provide free, no-obligation estimates based on your specific needs.

The time required depends on the size and condition of the area being cleaned. A typical driveway (500-600 sq ft) usually takes 1-2 hours. We'll provide a time estimate when we quote your project.

Not all surfaces can withstand the same pressure. We adjust our equipment settings based on the surface being cleaned. For delicate surfaces, we use lower pressure settings to ensure effective cleaning without causing damage.

As long as we have access to the areas that need cleaning and a water source, you don't need to be present during the service. Many of our customers prefer to be home for the initial consultation and then leave us to complete the work.

For most residential properties, we recommend power washing once a year to maintain appearance and prevent buildup of dirt, mold, and mildew. Properties with heavy tree coverage or in humid areas may benefit from cleaning every 6-9 months.

Yes, we use cleaning chemicals for most properties. When dirt and grime are heavily caked on surfaces, chemicals are used to achieve a consistent, thorough clean that our pressure washers alone cannot accomplish.

We take great care to protect your landscaping. The chemicals we use are diluted to reduce potential plant impact. However, we cover sensitive plants when necessary, and we always pre-wet and rinse vegetation before and after cleaning to minimize any effects on your garden.

We use Krud Kutter, a professional-grade cleaning solution that's particularly effective for cleaning driveways, concrete, brick, and masonry surfaces. This powerful cleaner excels at removing dirt, grime, oil stains, mold, mildew, and algae that can build up over time. It provides thorough cleaning while being more environmentally conscious than traditional bleach-based solutions.

We accept multiple payment methods for your convenience, including checks, cash, Venmo, and Apple Cash. If none of these options work for you, please contact us by email or speak with us on-site—we're happy to arrange an alternative payment solution.
